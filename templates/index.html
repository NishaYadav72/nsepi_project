Why NSDPI?

National Skill Development Program of India (NSDPI) is a premier National Training organization. Following are the special features of NSDPI:

National Skill Development Program of India (NSDPI) is a registered Brand.
Ministry of Corporate Affairs Reg. No: U80904DL2022NPL398502.
Ministry of Commerce and Industry, Trademark Reg. No: XX09X04
NITI AYOG NGO DARPAN Reg. No: DL/2022/0320041.
MSME Registration No: DL-02-0034537.
ISO 9001:2015 Quality Management Certificate No: 0722Q135522.
Start Up Registration No: DIPP22XXX.
Presently over 250 authorized training centers of National Skill Development Program of India (NSDPI) are running successfully in INDIA.
Till date more than 5,000 students have studied through various National Skill Development Program of India (NSDPI) Many students are successfully working in Govt. departments and some in private jobs and some are successfully running their own Training centers.
National Skill Development Program of India (NSDPI) has been awarded with ISO 9001:2015 quality certification.
National Skill Development Program of India (NSDPI) offers various courses of Computer & IT Education, Beautician and Fashion Designing, Teacher Training, Para Medical Courses, Amanat or Land Surveyor, Electrical and Machanical Training, 100+ Skill Development Training Program & Job Oriented Education, etc.
National Skill Development Program of India (NSDPI) courses have been accredited by Government Authorized assessment Agency.
National Skill Development Program of India (NSDPI) is launching a full fledged job portal to support its students for placement.
