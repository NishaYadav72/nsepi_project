Infrastructure Requirement

  • The franchise has to have minimum 800 Sq.ft. to 1200 sq.ft. area.
  • Excellent model of infrastructure which is Student friendly, Area should be 800 sqft to 1200 sqft or more.
  • There should be at least Two class room of size 200 to 250 sqft.
  • Office room should be approx 80 - 120 sqft
  • Recruiting of Teaching and administration staff.
  • Reception area should be wider enough for proper sitting arrangement of visitors
  • Clean & spacious toilet & washroom facility
  • Sitting capacity per class room - 40
  • One computer / Printer with scanner

Rules for Establishment of the Center

  • Application for establishment of Exam Center should be submitted within the date stipulated in the form.
  • The application should be submitted by itself and the institute Chairman/ Secretary or by the principal of the school/ college along with a declaration of non- judicial stamp paper worth Rs.10/-.
  • If the institute is recognized the application should be accompanied by the certified copy of the proposal, by laws and other certified copies of the testimonials.
  • The application should be attested by Gazetted officer or Notary.
  • The center opening fees is Rs. 10000/- (for two years) and center renewal fees will be Rs. 5000/- for every two years.

Examination / Study Center Rights & Responsibilities and Work

  • To process and distribute study & teaching materials.
  • To make arrangements for the exam and to appoint invigilators & examiners after obtaining approval from the NSDPI.
  • To collect Enrollment/Examination forms, exam material etc. from NSDPI Support office from time to time and keep it in safe custody and maintain confidentially.
  • Generally, Class for any course is conducted 2 hours per day.
  • Teachers Salary & Educational Institutes Rent are paid by the center-in-charge / Franchisee
  • For Successful running of Skill Development/Vocational course, all the advertisement expenses will be borne by the center-in-charge / Franchisee.
  • Centre incharge will deposit Rs. 1000/- (Rs. One Thousand only) per student per annum along with Admission form & examination fees of Rs. 650/- per head.
  • Centre-in-charge will submit the details of New admission within 30 days from the date of admission.
  • Centre in charge has to deposit the Examination fees of every student with Examination form till 5 May.
  • NSDPI will issue I-Card & Enrollment Number after receiving the Admission form with fees.
  • To Prepare statement of Registered Students coming for Study at the Centre and submit the same to the NSDPI from time to time and also to furnish the required documents for the inspection of the NSDPI representatives.
  • To make arrangements for conducting the exam of students transferred from another centre by the Franchisee/ Institute.
  • The centre in charge has to make the arrangements for the Practical Exams.
  • To observe scrupulously all instructions / orders issued by the Institute from time to time and do nothing which violate or come in the way of the code of conduct laid down by the NSDPI.
  • For any problem, the centre may contact the Head Office in person or by telephone on any working day.
  • Hindi is the recognized language in case of any controversy - English is just a translation.
  • Any centre violating the institute rules or working against the prestige will run the risk of its registration being cancelled without any prior notice, and all expenses there on will be borne by the centre incharge.
  • In the event of any controversy / disputes on amount of any student/ member/ employee of the centre or Institution will be resolved by the Committee appointed by the National Skill Development Program of India, New Delhi. Direct approach to the court will not be permissible; the decision of the committee is final. All disputes will be subjected to Delhi jurisdiction only.
