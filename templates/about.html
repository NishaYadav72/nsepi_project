About NSDPI

National Skill Development Program of India which is also known as NSD Program of India or NSDPI is Run by MPASS FOUNDATION. Which was established in the year 2022 an ISO 9001:2015 Certificate organisation. Incorporated under section 8, the ministry of Corporate Affairs, Govt. Of India Bearing the Registration Number: U80904DL2022NPL398502. Also Registered in the Ministry of MSME, Registration Number DL-02-0034537 & Registered under Niti Aayog Unique ID : DL/2022/0320041. National Skill Development Program of India has been awarded with an ISO 9001:2015 quality certification to Educational engaged in providing vocational education, skill Training and CSR course through its training centre in pan India known as the National Skill Development Program of India (NSDPI)



Vocational Traninig

NSD PROGRAME OF INDIA conducts industry-relevant and job- oriented vocational courses for aspiring youth. These courses are related to Electronics, Electrical, Telecom, Automotive, Mechanical, Information Technology, Apparel, Beauty and Wellness etc. The duration of these courses varies from 3 Months to 1 Year.

Corporate Traninig

NSD PROGRAME OF INDIA offers customized courses according to industry requirements to develop competency and improving the productivity of existing workforces. It's offering training need analysis, content development, training delivery at client location.

CSR Projects

NSD PROGRAME OF INDIA offers skill development programs funded by corporate / NGO as part of their CSR obligation which are specifically targeted at underprivileged youth. We create custom offerings that mobilize students, train them in the areas which have been mutually agreed on and empowering them to pursue sustainable livelihoods in the world of work.

Placements

NSD PROGRAME OF INDIA has an active placement cell and excellent linkages with various industries for placements. We work with partner industries and make sure all our students have job placement in the best companies.
